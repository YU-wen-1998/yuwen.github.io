Diary of a Schizophrenic

I designed this game to raise awareness of Schizophrenia, as well as mental health

A brief introduction to Schizophrenia

According to ChatGPT, schizophrenia is a complex mental disorder characterized by distorted thinking, hallucinations, delusions, disorganized speech, and impaired social functioning. It affects a person's perception of reality and can significantly impact their daily life.

Accurate diagnosis is vital to ensure appropriate treatment and support for individuals with schizophrenia.

But estimates from various studies suggest that the misdiagnosis rate for schizophrenia may range from 20% to 40%.

It can lead to incorrect treatment, delays in receiving proper care, and overlooking the unique symptoms of schizophrenia. This can result in worsening symptoms, lack of effective support, and increased self-endangerment.

So here I will introduce Diary of Schizophrenic.
