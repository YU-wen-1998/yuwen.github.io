Ball Maze

A simple Maze game.

I built this Maze Game with Unity3D.

Features

1. Move to collect Coins

You can move the ball to collect coins. When you collect a coin. There will be a sound effect. The number of coins you have collected will show on screen.

2. Hit wall panalty.

When you hit wall, you will lose HP.

You know you hit the wall when you hear the sound and see the effect.

When you lost all your HP, you will lose in an explosion. A "Game Over" will pop up.

3. Door control system

The door will open when you collect all the coins.
